CHARTWELL PARTNERS

Meet
the Firm

Chartwell Partners is a retained executive search and leadership advisory firm exclusively focused on serving the Financial Services industry. We partner with banks, financial institutions, and financial services organizations to identify and recruit senior executives and Board members who drive long-term performance, stability, and growth.

Our founding Partners left senior leadership roles at a global search firm to create a highly specialized, client-centric firm. Every assignment is led hands-on by a Partner and supported by an experienced research, execution, and administrative team.

Our Story Meet the Team

Our Story

Built on Relationships.
Driven by Results.

Chartwell Partners was founded by senior partners who left the largest global search firms with a singular purpose: to create a boutique firm where every search receives the attention it deserves, from the first conversation to successful onboarding.

Our founding partners bring decades of deep-domain expertise in Financial Services and Real Estate. Every engagement is led personally by a partner — never delegated to junior staff — ensuring our clients receive the highest caliber of service and insight.

We measure our success not only by placements made, but by the long-term performance and satisfaction of every leader we place. Over 80% of our business comes from repeat clients, a testament to the trust we've earned.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every search, every conversation, and every relationship we build.

Integrity

Our reputation is our livelihood. We choose to "think long" and let the golden rule govern every decision we make.

Excellence

We take pride in outstanding execution. Leveraging large-firm know-how, we apply a proven, disciplined search process with rigor and enthusiasm.

Partnership

We invest deeply in long-lasting relationships with clients and candidates alike, partnering through every step of the process — not just the placement.

Compassion

We chose this work because we genuinely enjoy watching our clients and candidates succeed. Caring deeply is where every great placement begins.
